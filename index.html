  1. FDM: Fused Deposition Modeling
    • The most common form of 3D printing
    • Melt plastic filament through a nozzle
    • Move the nozzle over three gantries using pulleys
    • Moove the nozzle as you melt the plastic, create something out of plastic wire
  2. Resin and UV light
    • Much simpler, but much more complicated
    • Fill a vat with UV-hardening resin
    • Place a high-resolution UV screen on the other side of a window at the bottom of the vat
    • Place a plate right next to the window, harden a layer of resin by displaying a cross section of an item
    • Lift the plate and repeat
    • UV resin is toxic and requires significant post-processing, this is where the difficulty is at
